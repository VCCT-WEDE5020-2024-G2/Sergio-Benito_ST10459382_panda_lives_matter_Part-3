Who We Are

The red panda is an elusive and mysterious mammal of the biodiverse Himalayan forests. They are also known as the "firefox," “lesser panda,” and “red-cat-bear.”

We prefer "first panda" and "original panda" because the red panda was described 50 years before the giant panda, and it gave pandas their name. (Red Panda Network).

Who is Panda Lives Matter?

Panda Lives Matter is a non-profit organization focused on raising awareness and supporting the conservation of red pandas. Our mission is to educate and foster a deep connection with these creatures, ensuring safe and sustainable habitats.
